About Us

Welcome to Temple Inn & Suites, your number one source for all things hotels. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of hotel service, with a focus on Church Temples, Weddings, and Family History.
Founded in 2005 by Isaias Zuniga, Temple Inn & Suites has come a long way from its beginnings in Bethesda, MD. When Isaias Zuniga first started out, his passion for hotel service drove him to do intense research, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a Global Hotel. We now serve customers all over Country, and are thrilled to be a part of the eco-friendly wing of the hotel industry.

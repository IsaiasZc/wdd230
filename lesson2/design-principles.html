Alignment

Khan Academy

khanacademy.org

In this webpage, I love the simplicity in the text and how its alignment with title, text, and button help us have consistency even with the image on the right side

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

apple.com

The white space is clear here, The intention is to highlight the iPhone and the text and image fulfill the purpose, the information is focused on what's the image conveys, there're no other text or color distracting from the main purpose

Repetition

UCV

ucv.edu.pe

The elements here share the same pattern to stay in harmony with each image, and the text has the same design: a descriptive icon, a highlight, and a short description. With the information displayed in this way, the user can easily understand the message.
